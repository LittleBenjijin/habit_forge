## How to Play

1. **Sign Up/Login**: Create an account to start your journey
2. **Create Tasks**: Add Habits, Dailies, and To-Dos
3. **Complete Tasks**: Earn XP and Gold by completing your tasks
4. **Level Up**: Progress through levels and increase your stats
5. **Shop**: Buy equipment to enhance your character
6. **Earn Achievements**: Complete challenges to unlock achievements
7. **Play Mini Games**: Earn extra gold through fun mini games

## Game Mechanics

### Rewards
- **Habits (+)**: 10 XP, 5 Gold
- **Habits (-)**: Lose 10 HP
- **Dailies**: 15 XP, 8 Gold
- **To-Dos**:
  - Trivial: 5 XP, 3 Gold
  - Easy: 10 XP, 5 Gold
  - Medium: 20 XP, 10 Gold
  - Hard: 40 XP, 20 Gold

### Death System
- When HP reaches 0, you lose 20 gold
- HP is fully restored after death
- Track your deaths in statistics
